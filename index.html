Beginner's Mind

Hello. Beginner's Mind is a mindset that aims to treat all things like you are new to them; like a beginner. It is said that by doing this, one approaches things like a child; pure, innocent, playfully, curiously. By approaching life in this way, one lets go of the need to conquer everything. Instead of treating everything does as something to be achieved, a skill to be mastered, or even something to base one's worth from, we can see things from a fresh lens. It is often said that the more learn, the less we know.
